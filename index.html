What we do:

K & G Law LLP is a law firm specializing in immigration and nationality law in the United States of America. Our experienced immigration lawyers are experts in this field of law and represent our clients in all areas.

• Family-based immigration and green cards– full legal options to sponsor your family members to obtain permanent residency in the United States. We will prepare all the necessary paperwork for the U.S. consulate or embassy interview abroad if you want to invite your loved one for just a visit to the USA by requesting a "visitor visa," or our green card lawyers will prepare a green card application on your family member's behalf.
